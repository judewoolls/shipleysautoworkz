Hi, I’m Charlie – founder of Shipley’s Autoworkz

I opened the garage in 2024. I’m a passionate car enthusiast with a background in drifting and motorsport. After years of hands-on experience and a love for everything automotive, I launched Shipley’s Autoworkz to bring top-quality service and expertise to car owners in the Bow/copplestone and Crediton area.

Whether you need an MOT, brake or clutch repairs, engine replacements, recovery, or a call-out – I’ve got you covered. I work on all types of vehicles, from show cars to daily drivers, with the same care and attention I give my own.

Looking forward to helping you get back on the road – and keeping you there.
